T4A Consent Form
Our records indicate you are not eligible to submit a T4A Consent Form.

Only students who were issued Scholarships, Bursaries and Awards in a prior tax year will be issued a T4A and are eligible to consent. Tuition tax credits are issued on a T2202 form and are available under My T2202A Slips in Student Center.

If you have questions about your T4A form contact our office at regtax@uwo.ca
